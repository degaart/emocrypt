emocrypt

Encrypt text into an emoji soup

This page will encrypt/decrypt your text locally in your browser without sending anything over the internet.

This is the web version of emocrypt, a free, open-source program that you can also download and use locally on your computer.

Downloadable versions: https://github.com/degaart/emocrypt/releases/

Source code: https://github.com/degaart/emocrypt
